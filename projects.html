The Forest

Overview

A previous project that I worked on in the group 'the Electric Chameleons' is 'The Forest' (not to be confused with the actual game 'The Forest' by Endnight Games - we couldn't think of any good names), a text-based survival adventure game made in Python. The premise is that you are in a warehouse (just kidding, you're in a forest) and you and your friend are being chased by a giant ravenous man-easting spider.
